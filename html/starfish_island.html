Visit Starfish Island

Starfish Island is a small uninhabited island located in Coron Bay. It is known for its clear, turquoise waters and the abundance of starfish that can be found on the sandy bottom. The island is only accessible by boat and is a popular spot for tourists looking to go snorkeling or diving. Visitors can swim among the colorful fish, corals and the starfish, which can be found in the shallow waters around the island. The best time to see starfish is during low tide.

It's important to note that while the island is called "Starfish Island" the island is also home to a wide range of marine life and coral, so visitors should be mindful of their actions and avoid touching or standing on the coral and marine life. Tour operators usually provide information about responsible behavior before the tour starts. This spot is definitely a must-see for those who enjoy snorkeling and diving, and want to see the beautiful marine life that the Philippines has to offer.
